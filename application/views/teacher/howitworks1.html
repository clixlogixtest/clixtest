This is how the online tutoring works

Single lessons with the latest technology

We can see each other, hear and talk

We write together on the digital tablet

We work on your book entries or worksheets

New - Decide without risk for one of our savings packages thanks to our Satisfaction Guarantee!

Tip - Create a user to arrange a non-binding trial lesson. Only test for free and then decide is Easy-Tutor no problem!

1. Finding the perfect teacher

SELECT A DESIRED TEACHER

Tip1 - Our teachers are so experienced that they can help all students. Whether at the middle school or at the gymnasium. No matter if in Bavaria or in NRW. With the help of one-to-one tuition, the individual curriculum of the student can be considered!

TIP2 - The purchased lesson times of our savings packages are credited to a time account that is valid for all teachers and subjects. Changing teachers or subjects is easily possible!

2. Choose a suitable date

SELECT AN APPOINTMENT THAT FITS PERFECTLY IN THE PERSONAL SCHEDULE

TIP - Whether it's the first trial lesson or the first paid appointments with us, appointments can easily be booked directly in the calendar of the desired teacher! Since only the available appointments of the teacher are visible, the appointment does not have to be confirmed additionally!

TIP - If you would like to book several appointments at the same time, you can click on Additional appointments and add additional tutoring sessions.

3. Complete appointment booking

FILL IN THE SHORT FORM

Attach the stapler to the teacher so he can prepare for the lesson

(You can take a picture of his notebooks and attach them directly to the appointment book, so that the teacher can prepare better! Just click on "Choose file"!)

Choose a form of payment

(The data goes directly to the payment mediator via SSL encryption.) No payment data is stored on our server.) Please note that no payment data is requested for the free trial lesson. If you want to book it and are surprised that you are asked for payment data, you are just in the wrong tab. Click on the tab "free trial lesson" and you're ready to go!)

Tip: save money and get additional benefits!

With the help of our savings packages, which can be booked completely risk-free thanks to our satisfaction guarantee, not only is the booking easier and more flexible, but you also receive a Wacom graphic tablet in the 50s and 100s package!

4. Start online tutoring

AFTER SUCCESSFUL BOOKING YOU WILL RECEIVE AN APPOINTMENT CONFIRMATION BY E-MAIL

(Please note: Only if you have also received this e-mail, the appointment will be confirmed)

Your tutor is just a click away from you. Please 5 minutes before the tutoring starts, click on the link in the email!

(Please note: There are real people sitting in front of the computer who would like to spend time with you and help you with your problems, simply not appearing at an appointment is unfriendly and unfair to the teacher!)

TIP - It is recommended to check the webcam and the microphone before, so that the tutoring can start smoothly. Both not available? Then you can also join the smartphone and use this as a replacement.

Easy-Cloud: Repeat everything again

ALL DOCUMENTS, WHICH WERE DEVELOPED WITH THE TEACHER, YOU CAN LOOK AGAIN

AFTER THE LESSON, THE TEACHER LOADS ALL CREATED DOCUMENTS INTO A PERSONAL FOLDER

TIP - To find the documents click on "Easy-Cloud" in the left bar. On the next page you have to go to the tab "Shared files" to see the teachers' folders. Here you can download all files.

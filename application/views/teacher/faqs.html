Online tutoring at Easy-Tutor - how does it work?

Easy tutor is individual tutoring. With all the advantages of one-to-one lessons, but with a few more important extras: As a platform, our students can freely choose their preferred teacher from a broad range of teachers. And most importantly: Easy-Tutor guarantees maximum freedom in he scheduling. The schedules of all teachers and their available times are visible. Because the tutoring by Easy-Tutor takes place online, annoying travel times are eliminated and lessons can even take place in the holiday home or at grandma's in the countryside. Here you can see how a tutoring session works.

Flexibility, spontanelty and simplicity are trumps. In addition, Easy-Tutor knows no expensive memberships, but only learning packages with different: Only the lessons that are really necessary, are booked and paid. This is how tuition works, which converts weaknesses into strengths

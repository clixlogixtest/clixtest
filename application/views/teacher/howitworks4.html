The Easy-Tutor Satisfaction Guarantee

Unsatisfied? Then we stop the payments!

We attach great importance to the satisfaction of our students and parents. Since the success in the classroom is dependent on many factors, we do not want to bind anyone who notices after some time that he has not found the right tutor with us.

That's why Easy-Tutor offers a 4-week satisfaction guarantee! If you realize in the first month of our 50 or 100 savings package that Easy-Tutor is not the right solution, then it is enough to send an e-mail to stop@easy-tutor.de and we will then terminate the membership, whereupon no further payments more mature.

However, there are a few rules here:

  • In the first four weeks, no more than 8 times 45 minutes may be booked. If exceeded, can not be resigned
  • With a resignation the credited minute credit is reduced to 8 times 45 minutes, which can still be used up.
  • The graphics tray provided by Easy-Tutor must be returned within 7 days of sending the cancellation email. If it is not returned, it must unfortunately be charged with 70 â‚¬.
  • It is a satisfaction guarantee and not a money back guarantee, as lessons are taken with real teachers who need to be paid.
  • Unfortunately, we can not make any exceptions, if you contact us only after expiration of the 4-week satisfaction guarantee.

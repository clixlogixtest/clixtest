About Easy-Tutor

Who are we and why do we do that?

"It just had to be found a good solution"

When Massimo Cancellara and Alexander Liebisch met at the "Faculty of Mechanical Engineering" at the Munich University of Applied Sciences, the subject of tutoring for both was initially just a job to finance the study of their future profession. The fact that after leaving school the job could not only become a profession, but even a company of its own was not foreseeable at first

On the other hand, for both the own work as a tutor the basis of the business idea for their startup: Munich is big and who as a tutor two students in different parts of the city visited in a row, spends more time on the street than with the tutoring. If you order your students, the problem will only be thrown on the youngsters with their tight schedules between long school days, violin lessons and sports clubs.

Her idea: "It would have to be much simpler, at the same time more concentrated and more efficient for everyone involved.

Massimo Cancellara explored the market for tutoring and as a teacher tested the strengths and weaknesses of various institutes. A market observation with the aim to offer the best model for tutoring. It was clear from the beginning - the best way to help students is personal one-to-one lessons.

The technical basis, according to Alexander Liebisch with many software tests, would have to be a videoconferencing system that offers maximum security, is easy to use and makes teaching materials available after class. The software that Easy-Tutor perfected for his tutoring solution is otherwise used as a conference tool in large companies - and children are more versed than many CEOs.

"We are now happy to help so many students."

Already today, Easy-Tutor has a virtual teacher's room with over 40 teachers and the student base of a medium-sized school. From their vision, Easy-Tutor grew into a highly functional platform that combines many benefits: The initial fear that online education could be less focused than on-site tutoring was unfounded. On the contrary, the medium turned out to be one of the biggest strengths. Students today are so technology-affine that the monitors of laptops or tablets even allow a distraction-free learning, The teacher is not sitting next to his students, but opposite them - with eye contact and the ability to use the monitor with a tablet as a common board. Each worksheet, every learning progress will be transferred to the Easy-Tutor-Cloud during the lesson, where the results will be accessible at any time afterwards.

Just as Easy-Tutor presents himself today, Massimo and Alexander have succeeded in transforming their core concern into a functioning system: a learning platform that more effectively supports parents and students - and that teachers and tuition students still meet each other directly. Just a whole new form of tutoring, which supports all the benefits of individual teaching with the technical resources of the Internet.With a perfect document storage for teaching material, with the ability to use the monitor not only as a classroom, but also as a blackboard. Easy-Tutor is so playful that it motivates students and at the same time so focused that no concentration gaps occur. The fact that it saves driving times of teachers, students and parents and can help even more quickly with flexible deadlines is a special bonus.

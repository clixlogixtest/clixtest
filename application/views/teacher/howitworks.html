Tip - Create a user account to arrange a non-binding trial lesson. The first lesson is free and you do not enter into a contract!

1. Finding the perfect teacher

SELECT A DESIRED TEACHER

Tip1 - We write together on the digital tablet

TIP2 - The purchased lesson times of our saving packages are credited to a time account that is valid for all teachers and subjects. Changing teachers or subjects is easily possible!

2. Choose a suitable date

SELECT AN APPOINTMENT THAT FITS PERFECTLY IN THE PERSONAL SCHEDULE

TIP - If you would like to book serveral appointments at the same time, you can click on > Further Dates < and add additional tutoring sessions

3. Complete appointment booking

FILL IN THE SHORT FORM

SEND PERSONAL INFORMATION TO THE TEACHER

(You can take pictures of his notebooks and make them available to the teacher so that he can prepare better)

CHOOSE A FORM OF PAYMENT

(The data goes directly to the payment broker via SSL encryption, no payment data is stored on our server)

Tip - Save additionally with Easy-Tutor

We recommend our savings packages with our satisfaction guarantee! After a purchase one receives a code, which one must enter at "Redeem voucher code". (Alternatively: e-mail address is also possible)

4. Start online tutoring

AFTER SUCCESSFUL BOOKING YOU WILL RECEIVE AN APPOINTMENT CONFIRMATION BY E-MAIL

5 MINUTES BEFORE THE TUTORING STRATS, CLICK ON THE LINK AND THE MEETING WITH THE TEACHER OPENS

TIP - It is recommended to check the webcam and the microphone before, so that the tutoring can start smoothly. Both not available? Then you can also join the smartphone and use this as a replacement.

Easy-Cloud: Repeat everything again

ALL DOCUMENTS, WHICH WERE DEVELOPED WITH THE TEACHER, YOU CAN LOOK AGAIN

AFTER THE LESSON, THE TEACHER LOADS ALL CREATED DOCUMENTS INTO A PERSONAL FOLDER

TIP - To find the documents click on "Easy-Cloud" in the left bar. On the next page you have to go to the tab "Shared files" to see the teachers' folders. Here you can download all files.

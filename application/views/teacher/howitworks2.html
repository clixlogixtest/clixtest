Students benefit from online tutoring. And her parents.

Anyone who thinks of tutoring still thinks most of the time about classroom tutoring: Usually the tutors look for the students or the students find an institute for their tutoring sessions. There are many good reasons to move the student tutoring to the Internet, as is now commonplace in many other services. Especially with the online tutoring system Easy-Tutor, which combines the many advantages of the Internet medium with those of the presence tutoring.

Professional online tutoring - not just pure mediation

Selection of tutors

Especially in rural areas, it is often not easy to find good and humane matching tutor teachers who have no long way to go for classroom hours. The choice among online tutoring is greater, because the students can choose independently regionally. For example, students at Easy-Tutor are more likely to find the right teacher, with whom they can understand each other well and with whom the collaboration also works humanly. Here you can arrange appointments with our teachers.

Online tutoring with Easy-Tutor offers more advantages than presence tutoring

Without media discontinuity, online tutoring allows teachers and students to work in concert with virtual media, playful methods, animated graphics and other resources offered by the Internet. The teacher can quickly respond with appropriate methods through direct conversation with his student if the student does not understand something. And by the way this form of tutoring also promotes the media literacy of the students. This is how Easy-Tutor works.

Focus and concentration

When learning online, students are focused and focused. External influences are hidden. They focus on sharing with their teacher and visualizing the learning content on the screen.

Foreign language tutoring with native speakers

Native speakers are a huge help in learning foreign languages. Via online tutoring, it is easy to take private lessons in French with a tutor who is a native speaker.

Students are much more motivated online

Young people are often and like online, feel at home on the computer and "at home". This is how online learning feels so natural to them, they often prefer to work online rather than using traditional media. And if learning is fun, it works better too.

The ideal room for restrained personalities

The 1: 1 learning situation between tutor and student leads to the fact that also introverted personalities can be optimally supported. The personal and yet on the monitor cooperation makes it easy for many to go out and get involved in the lessons.

Easy scheduling. Flexible time management.

Spontaneity and quantitative flexibility of tuition possible

Easy-Tutor not only books regular tutoring lessons online on a weekly basis, but also additional units for acute needs, such as math work that is all too often "tomorrow".

Easy Tutor offers hourly packages that allow you to book lessons whenever and in whatever discipline you need them. For example, if a particular topic is currently causing particular difficulties in understanding. Or if a longer homework assignment or a presentation has to be processed.

Tutors also possible for students who are permanently or temporarily not mobile

Even with illness or permanent immobility, students can participate in the online tutoring. Easy from home. Thus, a broken leg does not automatically lead to bad school grades. Even if classmates do not come by every day to discuss their homework.

Time savings

The time that tuition students and often their parents need to travel to or from tutoring can be used for other activities

Flexible time windows

Learning times and the duration of the lessons should be more flexible for online students than for on-site tutoring.

Easy-to-use media and tools

Good archiving of information

Good archiving of information In the Easy Tutor cloud, teachers and students save their documents, so that the students have access to their neatly filed documents at any time afterwards. Working with the online whiteboard at Easy-Tutor feels like the students are in the same room as your teacher. In addition, the archiving of the whiteboard entries ensures that information is archived for later, so that no paper is needed.

All required material is always ready

The students are in the online tutoring in their own rooms, so they always have all textbooks and materials ready, which could be needed during the tutoring. Forgot your worksheet? Not available any more. And if there is something to research or look up to during the lesson: lessons take place via the Internet. Both therefore always have access to the largest possible source of information.
